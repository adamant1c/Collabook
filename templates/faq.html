Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know to start your legend in Collabook.

🛡️ General & Accessibility

1. What exactly is Collabook?
Collabook is a next-generation text-based RPG powered by advanced artificial intelligence. Unlike traditional games with pre-set dialogues, here you can type *any* action, and the AI will narrate the consequences in real-time. It's a cross between a gamebook, a text adventure, and a D&D session with an always-available Dungeon Master.
2. Is it free? Are there hidden costs?
The core experience of Collabook is completely free. You can create an account, generate unlimited characters, and play in all available worlds without spending a dime. We support the project through non-intrusive ads (AdSense) to cover server and AI API costs.
3. Do I need to install anything to play?
No, Collabook is entirely browser-based. You can play from a PC, Mac, tablet, or smartphone without downloading anything. All you need is a stable internet connection. Your progress is saved to the cloud, so you can start on your computer and continue on your phone while traveling.
4. Can I play offline?
Since the core of the game relies on an AI processing your responses in real-time on our servers, an active internet connection is required to receive the Narrator's replies. However, data data usage is very low as it is primarily text.

⚔️ Gameplay & Mechanics

5. How does the combat system work?
Combat uses a turn-based system based on the D20 (a 20-sided die), similar to classic tabletop RPGs. When you attack, the system rolls a virtual die and adds your modifiers (Strength, Agility, etc.). If the total beats the enemy's Armor Class, you hit. The AI then describes the action cinematically. You can choose to attack, defend, use items, or attempt creative actions like "flipping the table on the orc".
6. What are Stats and how do they affect the game?
You have 4 main stats: Strength (physical damage, feats of strength), Agility (initiative, dodging, aim), Intelligence (magic, hacking, knowledge), and Charisma (persuasion, deception). High Charisma might let you talk your way out of a fight, while high Strength lets you smash through a locked door. Your choices should reflect your character's strengths.
7. How does saving work? Can I lose progress?
Saving is completely automatic. Every action you take is recorded in the database instantly. You don't need to worry about saving manually. If you close the browser or lose connection, you'll be right back at the last message received when you return.
8. What happens if my character dies?
Death in Collabook is part of the journey, not the end. Currently, if your character is defeated in combat, they will be "resurrected" at the nearest safe hub (or adventure start) with a penalty to Experience (XP) and gold. In the future, we will introduce an optional "Hardcore" mode with Permadeath for the bravest players.
9. How do I earn Experience (XP) and Gold?
You earn XP by overcoming challenges, defeating enemies, and most importantly, progressing the story. Gold is found as loot on enemies or as rewards for completing Quests. Use gold to buy better gear that will increase your chances of survival.

🌍 Worlds & Lore

10. Are the worlds connected?
Currently, the three worlds (Fantasy, Sci-Fi, Cyberpunk) are separate universes. Your character exists within the world they were created in. However, we are working on special "Crossover" events and a "Portal" system that might allow travelers to jump between dimensions in the future.
11. Does the AI always repeat the same things?
We use advanced language models (LLM) designed to avoid repetition. However, if you notice the narrator getting stuck or repeating a phrase, try taking a drastically different action to "unstick" the situation. We are constantly updating our models to improve variety and creativity.
12. Can I create my own content or worlds?
At the moment, worlds are curated by our team to ensure consistency and balance. However, we encourage players to submit their ideas for new factions, items, or scenarios via our Blog or by contacting us. The best ideas are often integrated into the official game!

🔒 Account, Safety & More

13. Is my data safe?
Absolutely. We collect only the essentials (email and username) to manage your account. We do not sell your data to third parties. We use encrypted connections (HTTPS) and follow GDPR guidelines for data protection. You can download or delete your data at any time.
14. Is it suitable for children?
Collabook is designed for a general audience, but given the generative nature of AI and themes of combat/adventure, we recommend supervision for users under 13. We have safety filters to prevent explicit content or excessive violence, but free interaction means results can vary.
15. How can I support Collabook?
The best way to support us is to play and share the game with friends! If you want to do more, disabling AdBlock on our site helps us keep the servers running. In the future, we will introduce donation options or Premium accounts with cosmetic perks.
16. How can I report a bug?
If you encounter a technical error or strange AI behavior, please use the contact form on the "Contacts" page or email us directly at mycollabook@gmail.com. Include as many details as possible (what you were doing, which browser you use) to help us solve the problem quickly.
17. What is the cookie policy?
We use essential technical cookies to keep your login active and third-party cookies (Google AdSense/Analytics) to show relevant ads and analyze traffic. You can manage your cookie preferences from your browser settings. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

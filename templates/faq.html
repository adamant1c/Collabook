{% trans "Getting Started" %}

{% trans "What is Collabook?" %}
{% blocktrans %} Collabook is an interactive storytelling platform where you can create unique characters, choose from various story worlds, and embark on collaborative narrative adventures. Your choices shape the story and determine your character's journey. {% endblocktrans %}
{% trans "How do I get started?" %}
{% blocktrans %} Simply create an account, design your character with unique attributes and background, select a story world that interests you, and begin your adventure. The platform will guide you through each step. {% endblocktrans %}
{% trans "Is Collabook free to use?" %}
{% blocktrans %} Yes, Collabook is currently free to use. You can create characters, explore worlds, and participate in stories without any cost. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Characters and Gameplay" %}

{% trans "Can I create multiple characters?" %}
{% blocktrans %} Yes, you can create multiple characters, each with their own unique attributes, backgrounds, and story progression. This allows you to explore different playstyles and narrative paths. {% endblocktrans %}
{% trans "What are the different story worlds available?" %}
{% blocktrans %} Collabook offers various story worlds spanning different genres including fantasy, sci-fi, mystery, and more. Each world has its own unique setting, challenges, and narrative possibilities. Visit the world selection page to explore all available options. {% endblocktrans %}
{% trans "How do choices affect my story?" %}
{% blocktrans %} Your choices directly influence the narrative direction, character relationships, and story outcomes. Different decisions lead to different paths, making each playthrough unique. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Account and Privacy" %}

{% trans "What information do you collect?" %}
{% blocktrans %} We only collect your username (not your real name) and email address. We are GDPR compliant and take your privacy seriously. For more details, please read our Privacy Policy. {% endblocktrans %}
{% trans "Can I delete my account?" %}
{% blocktrans %} Yes, you can request account deletion at any time by contacting us at mycollabook@gmail.com. We will permanently delete your account and associated data in accordance with GDPR regulations. {% endblocktrans %}
{% trans "How do I reset my password?" %}
{% blocktrans %} Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Technical Support" %}

{% trans "What browsers are supported?" %}
{% blocktrans %} Collabook works best on modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated to the latest version for the best experience. {% endblocktrans %}
{% trans "I found a bug. How do I report it?" %}
{% blocktrans %} Please contact us at mycollabook@gmail.com with a detailed description of the issue, including what you were doing when the bug occurred and any error messages you received. {% endblocktrans %}
{% trans "Is there a mobile app?" %}
{% blocktrans %} Currently, Collabook is a web-based platform accessible through your browser. While we don't have a dedicated mobile app yet, the website is mobile-friendly and works on smartphones and tablets. {% endblocktrans %}

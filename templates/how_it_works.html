How Collabook Works

Welcome to Collabook, the ultimate interactive RPG where your choices shape the narrative. Whether you are a seasoned role-player or a newcomer to the world of interactive fiction, this guide will walk you through every aspect of the game, from character creation to mastering the mechanics of our three unique worlds.

1. Getting Started

Collabook is designed to be accessible directly from your browser. No downloads are required. Simply create an account using your email or Google login, and you are ready to embark on your adventure. Your progress is saved automatically to the cloud, allowing you to pick up your story exactly where you left off, on any device.

Upon your first login, you will be greeted by the World Selection screen. Currently, we offer three distinct settings: Fantasy (high magic, dragons, kingdoms), Sci-Fi (space exploration, cybernetics, alien diplomacy), and Cyberpunk (neon cities, corporate espionage, hackers). Each world has its own unique rules, lore, and challenges.

2. Character Creation & Stats

Your avatar is your connection to the world. In the Character Creation phase, you will define not just your stats, but your identity. You will choose a name, a backstory, and allocate points to core attributes:

  • Strength: Determines your physical power and melee damage.
  • Agility: Affects your speed, evasion, and ranged accuracy.
  • Intelligence: Governs your magical aptitude, hacking skills, and problem-solving.
  • Charisma: Crucial for diplomacy, persuasion, and leadership.

These stats are not just numbers; they fundamentally alter the choices available to you. A high-strength character might be able to intimidate a guard, whereas a high-intelligence character could hack the security system to bypass them entirely.

4. Game Mechanics

Combat: When diplomacy fails, combat begins. Our turn-based system allows you to choose your strategy. Attack, defend, use items, or flee. Success depends on your stats and a random roll (D20 system). The AI narrator describes the outcome of your actions in vivid detail.

Loot & Inventory: Defeated enemies drop gold and items. You can use gold to buy better gear in town hubs. Potions, weapons, and armor are essential for surviving tougher encounters.

Experience (XP): Every successful action earns you XP. Leveling up grants you new stat points, making your character stronger and unlocking new abilities.

5. Interactive Storytelling & AI

At the heart of Collabook is our advanced AI storyteller. Unlike traditional games with pre-written dialogue, Collabook generates the story dynamically based on your inputs. If you want to try something unconventional—like trying to tame a dragon with a song instead of a sword—you can! Type your custom action, and the AI will determine the outcome based on your stats and the context.

This infinite possibility engine ensures that no two playthroughs are ever the same. Your choices have real consequences that ripple through the story. Save a village, and they might come to your aid later. Betray a faction, and they will hunt you down.

6. Community & Safety

We believe in a safe and inclusive gaming environment. Our AI is tuned to prevent explicit content, and our moderation team actively reviews reported incidents. You can share your adventures on our blog or social media. Join our community forums to discuss strategies, share lore, and meet fellow adventurers.
